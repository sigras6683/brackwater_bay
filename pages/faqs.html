About

How do I return a product?

With our 30 day return policy, you can return any product if you are not staisfied with it. If a product was purchased in store, bring the product and purchase receipt with you to our store to return the product. If you purchased a product online, neatly place your purchase in the packaging it was originally shipped in and return the package to the mailing address.

Why is BrackWater Bay different from other brands?

BrackWater Bay products focus on keeping our planet green and protecting endangered species in our marine and aquatic ecosystems. By supporting BrackWater Bay, you are also supporting our native marine species and beautiful ecosystem surrounding them.

Are your water bottles sturdy?

BrackWater Bay water bottles are made of stainless steel that is designed to withstand your outdoor activities and is scratch resistant.

How long will my order take to ship?

Due to our products being designed and created with the finest of care, shipping in the US can take 15-25 days.

Do I need an account to purchase products?

No, an account is not necessary in order to purchase products, but you will not earn rewards as customers with accounts will.
